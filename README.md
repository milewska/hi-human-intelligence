# HI — Human Intelligence

**A clothing brand and a reminder to trust the innate intelligence of the human body.**

Live at [humintelligence.org](https://humintelligence.org)

## About

HI lives at the intersection of movement, embodiment, and reclaiming the wisdom the body already has. It's a counter-signal to AI hype — not anti-tech, but pro-human.

Co-founded by Alex Milewski and [Celia Grannum-Perarnaud](https://www.theconsciousnest.com/celia/).
