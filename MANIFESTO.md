# HI — Human Intelligence: Manifesto

## The Message

**hi**

Welcome to Human Intelligence.

Join this movement of remembering and reclaiming the delicious awesomeness of BEING a homo sapien.

Take a deep breath, dive into an embodiment meditation, reach out and meet up, buy some gear, put it on, go out into the world, make eye contact, share a smile and spread the word....

**hi**

---

## The Vision

HI is a clothing brand and a reminder. A counter-signal to the AI hype cycle — not anti-tech, but fiercely pro-human.

We live at the intersection of:
- **Movement** — the body in motion
- **Embodiment** — presence, sensation, aliveness
- **Connection** — eye contact, smiles, real-world community
- **Reclamation** — trusting the innate intelligence the body already has

The brand is a nudge. A wink. A small act of remembering.

## The Stack

- **Frontend:** Static HTML/CSS/JS — Cloudflare Pages, deploys from `main`
- **Payments:** Stripe (Checkout + Webhooks)
- **Backend:** Netlify Functions (`/functions/`) + Python Flask (`/server/app.py`)
- **Domain:** [humintelligence.org](https://humintelligence.org)

---

## North Star

Every touchpoint — the site, the gear, the copy — should make someone feel more awake, more human, more willing to look up and connect.
